Helping Others by Providing Empowerment

The principal objectives of Caring Sister are as follows :

  1. To empower the less privileged financially under the platform of the gospel of Jesus.

  2. To assist striving petty traders with financial support including exposure to microfinance opportunities.

  3. To organise workshops and enlightenment programs for striving petty traders to imbibe the culture of saving and business expansion.

  4. To upholdthe assisted ones in prayers, morals support and spread the love of Christ through evangelism.

  5. We are concerned about helping others in the society and bringing smiles to the faces of every woman, child and man we caome across.
